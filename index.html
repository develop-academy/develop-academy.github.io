OVERVIEW

Website Development

M-F Mornings (9am-12pm)

Students will learn the basics of website development and create a customized personal website from scratch by the end of the week. Web server and hosting costs are complementary.

The course starts with an introduction to HTML and covers everything from HTML 5 semantic elements to CSS 3 responsive website design. No prior experience is necessary.

Leadership Development

M-F Afternoons (1pm-4pm)

Students will develop their leadership and public speaking skills. We strongly believe that leaders are made, not born. Every student will write, memorize, and deliver a minute long speech at the end of the week.

The course starts with an introduction to the fundamental principles of proactive leadership and persuasive speaking. Students will also learn personal and time management.
